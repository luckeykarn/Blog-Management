OUR STORY

From Idea to Impact

Founded in 2020

ModernBlog began as a small passion project between three friends who wanted to document their learning journey in web development. What started as casual notes quickly grew into a full-fledged publication as readers resonated with our authentic approach to complex topics.

Today, we reach over 500,000 monthly readers across 120 countries, but we've never lost our original spirit of curiosity and knowledge-sharing.
